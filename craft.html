Our Craft

The jacket is the easy part.

The leather takes weeks. The skill takes years. Here's what goes into one Second Skin.

01 — The Leather

Top 5% full-grain. No exceptions.

We reject corrected and bonded leather entirely. Every hide is full-grain — the strongest, most breathable layer — hand-graded for character, then vegetable-tanned over weeks using bark tannins rather than rushed in chemical baths. It's slower, costlier, and the only way to get leather that ages instead of cracks.

02 — How It's Made

One jacket. One maker.

Cut

Each panel is hand-cut to follow the natural grain, so the leather moves with the body, not against it.

Sew

A single maker sews the whole jacket — no assembly line — with lockstitched, stress-tested seams.

Finish

YKK Excella hardware, hand-set rivets, and a final inspection against a 40-point standard.

03 — Leather Care

Built to be kept.

A great leather jacket should outlive its trends. Condition it twice a year, keep it on a wide hanger, let rain dry naturally — and full-grain leather will reward you with a patina no new jacket can fake. Care questions? Ask us anything, any time.
